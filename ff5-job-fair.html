Rules

  1. Character must remain a freelancer until at least one of the jobs is available.
  2. Character may switch freely between the 2 jobs until one job is mastered.
  3. Once a job is mastered, the character must use their other job until it is also mastered.
    • If one job is mastered but the other job is unavailable, chracter must remain as the mastered job until the other job is available.
    • Freelancer job is always considered “mastered”, so if the character has a freelancer job they must use the other job once available unil mastered.
  4. Once both jobs are mastered, character may switch freely between the 2 jobs.
  5. This is just for fun. Feel free to alter the rules to make it more fun.
